by Janey Thompson

I love movies. I love costumes. I love quirky or unusual costumes in movies that are not necessarily considered "costume flicks".

This is where Robert De Niro and Martin Scorsese enter the picture (so to speak). My background in costume design for theatre means that my mind is forever picking apart costume choices in movies and the ways in which clothing communicates character. For me, the dark, violent nature of Martin Scorsese's films makes the costumes worn by Robert De Niro all the more interesting.
